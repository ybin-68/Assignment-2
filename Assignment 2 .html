How to create a good visual design

What is visual design?

1.Definition of visual design

Visual design focuses on enhancing the aesthetic and usability of a digital product. It is the strategic implementation of images, colors, fonts, and layouts. Although many visual design elements deal with the look of a product, the feel of the product is equally important.

2.Elements of visual design

  • Line
  • Shape
  • Color
  • Texture
  • Form
  • Space
  • Tone
